Why ducks?

They are very cute and cool
They are loyal friends

Famous ducks

Wrinkle the duck

This is probably the classic image when you imagine the word duck. Wrinkle, also known as Seducktive, is a self-described professional duck. She has her blog on Instagram, where she has 629k followers and talks about her travels. This cutie is also known for running the New York City Marathon, as well as her red shoes.

Fatty and friends

Fatty and friends is a group of 4 cute ducks: Fatty, Cutie, Mocha and Latte. They are all different in age and color, but one thing can be said: they are all living their best lives. With their Instagram blog, which has 70k followers, they often post about their lives and travels and often share interesting thoughts.

Callduck Kira

Callduck Kira is a bright, neat and very cute duck from Japan. On her Instagram blog, you can see many posts about her everyday life, as well as many funny photos and videos about the life of a little duck in a big metropolis.
